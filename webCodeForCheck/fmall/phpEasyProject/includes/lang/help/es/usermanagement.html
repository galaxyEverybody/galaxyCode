User Management

The area “User Management” is only for the Administration (Direction) available. The construction and the service of staff members list and the editor-mask have been developed with same principle, as at all other areas.

Clicking on you labeling-cells in the list-table sorts from - and away the whole list after selected column. Editing is reloaded with the mouse-click on certain entry in editor-mask. Deactivating of co-worker-account makes impossible this co-worker from immediately the access for phpEasyProject.

Also the ascription to different projects works like with the project-administration with the double click on the wished project of a list to the other.
